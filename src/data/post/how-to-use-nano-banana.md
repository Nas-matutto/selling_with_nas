## Case 5: Different Backgrounds and Settings 
**Swap Backgrounds to Match Campaigns and Seasons**

One of the biggest challenges in e-commerce is keeping your visuals fresh. Customers want to see products in **different settings** that match their lifestyle. Nano Banana helps you:  

- Drop your product into **seasonal themes** (snowy winters, tropical summers).  
- Tailor visuals for different regions (urban lofts for NYC, villas for Dubai).  
- Save on location costs and logistics.  

**Example:** A candle brand can showcase their product on cozy winter tablescapes or airy summer patios - all with a few clicks. This makes your Shopify store look updated and relevant year-round.



## Case 6: Different Angles 
**Generate Product Shots from Every Angle**

Shoppers are more likely to buy when they can see a product from multiple perspectives. Normally, this means reshooting your product endlessly. With AI, you can:  

- Rotate your product digitally to show **front, side, and back views**.  
- Highlight details like zippers, textures, or packaging labels.  
- Create 3D-like presentations for a premium product experience.  

**Example:** A backpack brand can display 360-degree views without hiring a specialized photographer. This directly improves **conversion rates on Shopify product pages**.



## Case 7: Remove People or Things from Your Shoots 
**Clean Up Product Photography Instantly**

Sometimes your best photos get ruined by distractions like an unwanted hand, a shadow, or clutter in the background. With Nano Banana, a simple prompt can help you edit the picture to either add or remove extra elements from it. You now don't have to worry about "wasting" a photo just because it contains people (or other brands) that make it unusable.

**Example:** A skincare brand can remove bottles in the background to highlight only their hero product. This reduces the need for **reshoots and costly editing software**.



## Case 8: Marketing Materials 
**Turn Product Shots into Billboards, Posters, and Ads**
